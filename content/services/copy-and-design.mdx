An email that reaches the inbox but doesn't get read is worth the same as one that went to spam.

Copy and design are where the revenue actually comes from — but only if they work together. A beautiful template with weak copy doesn't convert. Sharp copy in a poorly rendered email doesn't either. We do both in-house so neither gets compromised.

## Copy

Every email we write starts with a brief: who is receiving this, what do we want them to do, and what's the one thing they need to believe to do it?

From there:
- **Subject lines** — written to earn the open, not trick it. We test variations where volume supports it.
- **Preview text** — the second subject line most brands ignore. We write it.
- **Body copy** — written in the brand's voice, at the right reading level for the audience. Short paragraphs, clear hierarchy, one primary action per email.
- **CTAs** — specific and action-oriented. "Shop the collection" says more than "Click here."

We don't use templates or AI generation. Every email is written from scratch for the specific audience and objective.

## Design

Mobile-first. At least 60% of your subscribers are reading on a phone — we design for that first.

We build in your brand's visual language: typography, colour, imagery style, spacing. We keep templates clean and fast-loading. We make the hierarchy obvious — the reader shouldn't have to work to find the main point or the CTA.

Every template is tested across Gmail, Apple Mail, and Outlook before it goes near a live list.

## What we don't do

We don't use off-the-shelf template libraries. We don't outsource copy to a freelancer and design to a different one. We don't treat email as a banner ad — words matter, and we take them seriously.
