Your email platform is the infrastructure everything else runs on. A poorly configured account — with broken integrations, misfiring triggers, incorrect segment logic, or a tangled flow structure — means campaigns go to the wrong people and revenue gets left behind.

Getting it set up correctly once is worth more than any single campaign.

## Platforms we work with

**Klaviyo.** Our primary platform for e-commerce. Deep Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce integrations, purpose-built segmentation, and flow logic that responds to real purchasing behaviour. If you're selling products online and not on Klaviyo, it's worth a conversation about whether you should be. [See how we work with Klaviyo →](/klaviyo-agency-australia)

**HubSpot.** Strong choice for B2B, SaaS, and services businesses with a longer sales cycle. We handle marketing hub setup, lifecycle stage configuration, workflow builds, and integration with your CRM.

**Customer.io.** Flexible and powerful for product-led SaaS. We configure event-based triggers, user attribute syncs, and behavioural campaign logic that maps to your product's activation and retention milestones.

**Mailchimp and Brevo.** We work with both where they're the right fit — typically smaller lists, simpler segmentation needs, or where a migration isn't yet justified.

## Migrations

Moving platforms is disruptive if it's not managed properly. We've handled migrations from Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Omnisend, Dotdigital, and various legacy ESPs.

Every migration covers:
- **List export and cleaning** — subscribers, suppressions, and historical data
- **Segment recreation** — rebuilding your segments in the new platform's data model
- **Flow rebuilding** — not copy-pasting, but rebuilding flows to use the new platform's logic correctly
- **Sending domain setup** — authentication records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), dedicated sending domains
- **Warm-up plan** — phased send volume ramp to protect deliverability through the transition

## Integrations and data

An email platform is only as useful as the data flowing into it. We set up and troubleshoot integrations between your ESP and your store, CRM, product database, and any third-party tools — loyalty programs, review platforms, subscription management — that should be informing your sends.
