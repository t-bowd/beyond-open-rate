Queensland's business landscape has changed quickly. Brisbane's e-commerce and services sectors have grown substantially in the past few years, and the brands doing well are increasingly investing in owned channels — email chief among them.

The reason is straightforward: paid social and search have gotten more expensive and less predictable. Email is the counter-balance. Once the list and the infrastructure exist, the economics are consistent.

## What we handle

**The core automation flows.** For most Brisbane businesses we work with, the immediate opportunity is in flows that aren't live or aren't performing: the welcome series that introduces the brand properly, the abandoned cart that recovers revenue you're already earning, the post-purchase sequence that turns one-time buyers into repeat customers.

We build these correctly from the start — with the right trigger logic, the right audience exclusions, and copy that reflects the brand — and then tune them each month against revenue data.

**Campaign management.** A consistent monthly campaign calendar, segmented and sent to the right people. Promotions, launches, seasonal content. We run the full execution — copy, design, build, send — so there's no ongoing load on your team.

**Deliverability and list health.** A lot of growing businesses accumulate list problems over time — disengaged contacts, poor authentication setup, inconsistent sending patterns — and don't notice until deliverability quietly degrades. We catch and fix these before they become a revenue problem.

**Reporting.** Monthly reporting tied to revenue — what email contributed, which flows are performing, what the next move is. Clear enough that you can act on it without needing to interpret it.

## Getting started

The fastest path is a brief scoping call — 30 minutes to understand your current setup, list size, and what you're trying to achieve. From there we'll either propose a standalone audit or a build-and-manage engagement, depending on where you are.

Most clients see their first new or rebuilt flow live within two weeks of starting.
