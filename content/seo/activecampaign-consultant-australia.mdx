ActiveCampaign is one of the more capable platforms in the mid-market: solid automation builder, useful CRM features, and a flexible contact model that works for businesses with longer and more complex sales cycles.

But it's also easy to accumulate a tangle of overlapping automations, neglected lists, and deliverability problems that nobody noticed building up. Most ActiveCampaign accounts we work with were set up correctly at some point — and then weren't touched as the business grew.

## What we do

**Automation audit and rebuild.** We map your existing automation architecture: what's active, what's triggering correctly, what's firing on the wrong contacts, and what's missing entirely. We then fix and rebuild in priority order — the automations closest to revenue first.

**List hygiene.** Disengaged subscribers drive up complaint rates and suppress deliverability. We clean the list, build sunset automation (automatic suppression of long-inactive contacts), and set up engagement-based segmentation so your broadcasts land in the inbox.

**Deliverability.** We check SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configuration, review sending domain setup, and fix authentication gaps. If you've had a deliverability incident, we identify the cause and build a remediation plan.

**CRM and pipeline setup.** For B2B businesses, we configure contact scoring, deal pipelines, and the automation triggers that connect marketing activity to your sales process.

**Campaign management.** Regular email campaigns — planned, written, designed, and sent. ActiveCampaign's visual builder and conditional content blocks let us do more personalisation per send than most simpler platforms allow.

**Platform migrations.** If ActiveCampaign isn't the right fit for where your business is going, we'll tell you — and handle the migration to a platform that is. For e-commerce brands, that usually means Klaviyo. For larger B2B teams, it's often HubSpot.

## Whether to stay or migrate

If you're using ActiveCampaign for a B2B or service business with a genuine sales process, it's probably the right platform. Get it working properly before considering a switch.

If you're using it for an e-commerce business — particularly one running on Shopify — the gap in native e-commerce data integration is real. Klaviyo's browse abandonment triggers, product feed integration, and purchase-based segmentation are materially more powerful for that use case.

We'll tell you which situation you're in before we recommend anything.
