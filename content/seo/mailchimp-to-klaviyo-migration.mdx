Mailchimp works. But if you're running an e-commerce business with Shopify or WooCommerce and you're serious about email as a revenue channel, you're leaving significant money on the table.

The gap isn't just features — it's data. Klaviyo is built around behavioural and purchase data. Mailchimp isn't. That difference determines what automations you can run, how precisely you can segment, and how much of your revenue email can actually claim credit for.

## What the migration covers

**Data export and cleaning.** We pull everything out of Mailchimp: subscriber list, custom fields, tags, segments, and suppression list. Before we import anything to Klaviyo, we clean the list — removing invalid emails, flagging long-inactive subscribers, and structuring the data to match Klaviyo's contact model.

**Suppression list transfer.** Your unsubscribes and complaint records come with you. This is non-negotiable for compliance — we make sure every opt-out from Mailchimp is respected in Klaviyo from day one.

**Sending domain setup.** We configure your sending domain in Klaviyo and set up or verify SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records. If you're moving to a dedicated sending domain (recommended for high-volume senders), we handle that setup.

**Flow rebuilding.** We don't copy-paste your Mailchimp automations into Klaviyo. We rebuild them to take advantage of Klaviyo's trigger logic — browse abandonment, cart abandonment with dynamic product blocks, post-purchase sequences based on product category. If your Mailchimp automations were simple, this is where you immediately start to see the difference.

**Warm-up plan.** Moving to a new ESP means starting fresh on sender reputation for that domain. We run a phased warm-up — increasing send volume gradually while monitoring deliverability — so you don't take a reputation hit at the moment you most need performance.

**Integration connection.** We connect Klaviyo to your Shopify, WooCommerce, or other e-commerce platform and verify that order data, product feeds, and behavioural events are flowing correctly before you send anything.

## What you get

A fully operational Klaviyo account with your list clean, your flows rebuilt for Klaviyo's data model, your sending domain authenticated, and a warm-up plan that protects deliverability through the transition. Most brands see improved results within the first month — more from better trigger logic than from any single campaign.
