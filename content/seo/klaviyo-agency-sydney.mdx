Sydney is home to a large share of Australia's e-commerce brands — from fashion and beauty to food, homewares, and specialty retail. Most of them are on Klaviyo. Far fewer of them are getting close to what their Klaviyo account is capable of.

The gap is almost always the same: a welcome series and an abandoned cart flow built at launch, broadcasts going to the full list regardless of engagement, and no visibility into what email is actually contributing to revenue.

## What most Sydney Klaviyo accounts are missing

**Browse abandonment.** Consistently underbuilt, consistently high-performing. When a subscriber browses a category or product and leaves without adding to cart, that's a clear signal. A well-timed browse abandonment email — with the right framing and product — brings a meaningful percentage back. Most accounts don't have this flow at all.

**Segmentation.** Sending the same broadcast to your entire list is one of the fastest ways to damage deliverability and depress revenue per recipient. We build engagement-based segments (active, lapsing, inactive), RFM tiers (high-value, at-risk, lapsed), and product category buyers — and we use them to make every send more relevant.

**Post-purchase sequences.** The moment after purchase is when trust is highest. A good post-purchase flow includes: a review request at the right time, a cross-sell based on what they bought, a replenishment reminder for consumable products. Most accounts have an order confirmation and nothing else.

**Reporting that shows revenue.** Klaviyo's default dashboard shows opens and clicks. What you want to see is revenue per recipient by send, contribution to total revenue by month, and flow performance broken down by email. We build reporting that gives you that view.

## What we do

**Flow builds and optimisation.** We build or rebuild the core flows — welcome series, browse abandonment, cart abandonment, post-purchase, win-back — with correct trigger logic, audience exclusions, and copy that converts. We A/B test sequences, subject lines, and send timing systematically.

**Campaign management.** A monthly calendar of broadcast campaigns: planned, written, designed, and sent. Each send is segmented by engagement and purchase behaviour. We own the cadence so you're not thinking about what to send next month.

**Klaviyo migrations.** If you're moving to Klaviyo from another platform, we handle the full process: data export and cleaning, segment recreation, flow rebuilding, domain authentication, and a phased warm-up plan.

**Deliverability.** SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configured correctly. List hygiene on a regular cadence. Sunset automation for long-inactive subscribers. Monitoring that catches problems early.
