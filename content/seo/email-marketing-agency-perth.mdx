Perth and Western Australia represent a significant and often underserved market for e-commerce and B2B businesses. The timezone difference from the east coast has historically pushed Perth businesses toward local providers — but for a channel like email, what you need isn't geographic proximity. It's strategic depth.

Email marketing is one of the highest-ROI channels available to Australian businesses regardless of location. The mechanics are the same whether you're in Perth, Sydney, or Brisbane: lifecycle automation that earns while you sleep, campaigns segmented by behaviour and purchase history, and a sender reputation that keeps you in the inbox.

## What we build for Perth businesses

**Lifecycle automation.** The flows that run without intervention — welcome series, abandoned browse and cart, post-purchase sequences, win-back campaigns. For most e-commerce brands, these five to six flows represent the bulk of what email can earn. We build them correctly from the start and optimise them over time.

**Campaign management.** A planned monthly calendar of broadcast campaigns: seasonal, promotional, and content sends. Segmented by engagement and purchase history so you're not burning the list with irrelevant bulk sends.

**Platform setup.** If you're on Klaviyo, HubSpot, or another platform and it hasn't been set up properly, we fix it. If you're migrating from Mailchimp or another legacy tool, we handle the full transition.

**Deliverability.** SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configured. List hygiene on a regular cadence. Complaint rate monitoring. If your emails are landing in spam or your open rates have dropped suddenly, we diagnose and fix the cause.

**Reporting.** Monthly reporting built around revenue per recipient and contribution to total revenue — not open rate and click rate. If you can't tell what email is actually earning, we build reporting that shows you.
