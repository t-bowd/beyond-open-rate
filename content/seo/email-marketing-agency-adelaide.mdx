Adelaide businesses are increasingly competing nationally and internationally — particularly in e-commerce, food and beverage, and professional services. Email is one of the channels that scales with you regardless of where your customers are.

The fundamentals are the same for any Australian business: lifecycle automation that earns on autopilot, campaigns that land in the inbox rather than the spam folder, and reporting that shows what email is actually contributing to revenue.

## What we build for Adelaide businesses

**Lifecycle automation.** The sequences that run without manual effort — welcome series, abandoned browse and cart flows, post-purchase nurture, and win-back campaigns. For most e-commerce brands, these flows represent the most reliable email revenue. We build them to best practice and optimise them over time.

**Campaign management.** Monthly broadcast campaigns planned, written, designed, and sent. We build a 30-day calendar each month covering promotions, launches, and content — and we segment each send so it's relevant to the people receiving it.

**Klaviyo and platform setup.** If you're not getting the most from your current ESP, we audit the account and fix it. Klaviyo in particular has a depth of segmentation and automation capability that most accounts barely use. If you're on Mailchimp or another legacy tool and ready to migrate, we handle the full transition.

**Deliverability.** Authentication records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) set up correctly. List hygiene cadence to keep complaint rates low. Monitoring to catch issues early. For businesses that have had deliverability problems — spam folder placement, blacklisting — we diagnose the cause and build a remediation plan.

**Reporting.** Revenue per recipient, flow performance, and contribution to total revenue. We give you a monthly view that tells you whether the program is working — not just what the open rate was.
